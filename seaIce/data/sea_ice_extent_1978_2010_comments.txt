Daily values of Northern Hemisphere sea ice extent, zonal-mean ice
edge latitude, and equivalent extent (defined as total surface area,
including land, north of the zonal-mean sea ice edge latitude). The
columns are as follows: (1) year, (2) month, (3) day, (4) ice extent
(10^6 km^2), (5) ice edge latitude (degrees N), (6) equivalent extent
(10^6 km^2).

Reference: Eisenman, Ian (2010), Geographic muting of changes in the
Arctic sea ice cover, Geophys. Res. Lett., 37, L16501,
doi:10.1029/2010GL043741.
